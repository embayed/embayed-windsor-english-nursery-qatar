Communication & Language

We develop this area in the circle time. Our kids use some words, symbols & signs to communicate; they recognize letters, their shapes and sounds. In the circle time, kids are able to develop their knowledge and understanding as well as developing confidence to say their names and talk about their experience. We work on improving children’s concentration, listening and speaking skills in group activities.
