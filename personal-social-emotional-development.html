Personal, Social & Emotional Development

By creating a positive approach to learning and finding out about the world around them, children are able to develop confidence in themselves; they value their own achievements and learn how to play in group in a safe and fun environment. In turn, children will make friendship and form good relationships with the adults they are working with.
